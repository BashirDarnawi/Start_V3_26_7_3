Privacy Policy — Albayan

Effective date: 17 July 2026 · Contact: bashirdernawi1999@gmail.com

To ask us to delete your Albayan account, use the public account-deletion request page.

What Albayan is

Albayan is a business management application (web, iOS and Android) used by advertising-office teams to manage customers, advertisements, receipts, deliveries and related business records.

Information we collect

  • Account information: your name, email address and a securely hashed form of your password. We do not store your plain-text password.
  • Business records you create: customers, advertisements, receipts, payments, delivery information and any photos you attach (for example receipt or advertisement photos).
  • Technical and audit logs: basic request information (such as time, IP address and browser type) and an in-app history of who created or changed a record, used for security, troubleshooting and accountability.

How your information is used

Your data is used to provide the app's features to you and your team: signing in, showing and syncing your team's business records, protecting the service, troubleshooting problems and keeping an audit history. We do not sell your data, show advertising, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

Where your information is stored

  • Server mode: Albayan's current server infrastructure is hosted through the Libyan Spider JPaaS (Jelastic) platform. Data is transmitted between the app and the server over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • Local mode: when the application is deliberately configured to use local storage instead of server mode, its records remain in that browser or device storage unless a user exports or transfers them.

Data retention and account deletion

Business records are generally kept while the team's workspace is active. Audit logs are retained for security and accountability and removed through the server's configured retention cleanup. The current default retention setting is 90 days, but an administrator may configure another permitted period, and the actual removal date depends on when operational cleanup runs. Operational backups may remain until their normal rotation expires.

When an administrator deletes an account, sign-in access is disabled. After a verified deletion request, an administrator can run Albayan's privacy-anonymization process to replace the account's name, email address and credentials with non-identifying values and remove its active sessions and password-reset records. Because Albayan is a shared business-record system, advertisements, receipts, payment records and other records belonging to the workspace are not automatically erased. The non-identifying internal account id and audit references may be retained when reasonably needed to preserve record integrity, investigate abuse, protect the service, or meet legal, tax or accounting obligations.

You may submit a verified request to delete or anonymize personal information that is not required for those purposes. Requests are reviewed rather than processed automatically. We will explain what action was taken and identify any information that must be retained. Submit a request through the account-deletion request page or email bashirdernawi1999@gmail.com.

Your rights

You can request access to your personal data, correction of inaccurate personal data, or deletion of eligible personal data using the contact options above. We may ask you to verify that you own the account before acting on a request.

Children

Albayan is a business tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be published at this address with an updated effective date.

المعلومات التي نجمعها

كيف نستخدم معلوماتك

تُستخدم بياناتك لتقديم ميزات التطبيق لك ولفريقك، مثل تسجيل الدخول وعرض سجلات العمل ومزامنتها وحماية الخدمة وحل المشكلات وحفظ سجل التدقيق. نحن لا نبيع بياناتك ولا نعرض إعلانات ولا نشاركها مع أطراف ثالثة لأغراض تسويقية.

أين تُخزَّن معلوماتك

  • وضع الخادم: تتم استضافة البنية التحتية الحالية لخوادم البيان عبر منصة Libyan Spider JPaaS (Jelastic). تُنقل البيانات بين التطبيق والخادم عبر اتصالات HTTPS مشفّرة.
  • الوضع المحلي: عندما يُضبط التطبيق عمداً على استخدام التخزين المحلي بدلاً من وضع الخادم، تبقى سجلاته داخل ذلك المتصفح أو الجهاز ما لم يصدّرها المستخدم أو ينقلها.
