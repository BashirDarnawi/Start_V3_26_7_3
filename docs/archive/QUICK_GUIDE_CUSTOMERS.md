## 📋 Sort Options Explained

| Option | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Newest First** | Recently added customers appear first |
| **Oldest First** | Oldest customers appear first |
| **Last Active** | Customers who made ads recently appear first |
| **Highest Paid** | Customers who paid the most money |
| **Lowest Paid** | Customers who paid the least money |
| **Most Spend** | Customers who spent the most on ads |
| **Least Spend** | Customers who spent the least on ads |
| **Biggest Credit** | Customers with the most prepaid balance |
| **Highest Debt** | Customers who owe the most money |

---

## 💰 Financial Filter Options

| Filter | Shows Customers With |
|--------|---------------------|
| **All Financials** | All customers (no filter) |
| **Has Credit** | Positive balance (prepaid) |
| **Has Debt** | Negative balance (owes money) |

## 🎨 Color Meanings on Customer Cards

| Color | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 🟢 **Green** | Customer has credit (prepaid) |
| 🔴 **Red** | Customer has debt (owes money) |
| ⚫ **Gray** | Balance is zero (even) |
| 🔵 **Blue** | Number of linked pages |
| 🟣 **Indigo** | Platform type |

## 🔍 Common Use Cases

### Finding Customers Who Owe Money:
1. Click "All Financials" dropdown
2. Select "Has Debt"
3. Sort by "Highest Debt"
4. Contact top customers first

### Finding Your Best Customers:
1. Sort by "Highest Paid (Revenue)"
2. Review top customers
3. Plan special offers or rewards

### Finding Inactive Customers:
1. Sort by "Last Active (Recently)"
2. Scroll to bottom
3. See customers who haven't been active
4. Plan re-engagement campaigns

### Managing Prepaid Balances:
1. Filter by "Has Credit"
2. Sort by "Biggest Credit Balance"
3. Remind customers to use their credit

## 📊 Understanding the Balance

```
Balance = Paid (Receipts) - Spent (Ads)
```

### Examples:

**Customer A:**
- Paid: 1000 LYD
- Spent: 800 LYD
- **Balance: +200 LYD** (Credit - shown in green)
- → Customer has prepaid 200 LYD

**Customer B:**
- Paid: 500 LYD
- Spent: 700 LYD
- **Balance: -200 LYD** (Debt - shown in red)
- → Customer owes 200 LYD

**Customer C:**
- Paid: 600 LYD
- Spent: 600 LYD
- **Balance: 0 LYD** (Even - shown in gray)
- → Customer account is settled

## ⚡ Quick Tips

1. **Search is Smart**: Search by name, phone, or platform
2. **Real-Time Updates**: Stats update automatically when you add/edit records
3. **Combine Filters**: Use search + sort + filter together
4. **Last Ad Date**: See "Never" for customers without ads yet
5. **Linked Pages**: Badge shows how many pages are linked to each customer
